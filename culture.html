An important part of my identity is my culture. I am proudly Mexican! I visit Mexico often and engage in the culture as much as I can. I prioritize staying connected through holidays, food, music, and the language.

What are important aspects of Mexican culture? See the images below!

Music! This is Harvard's very own mariachi, called Mariachi Veritas.

Holidays! This is a traditional ofrenda set up for Dia de Los Muertos. This is my personal favorite holiday, as it makes me feel connected to my ancestors. This holiday honors the balance of life and death, welcoming deceased loved ones back into the land of the living.

Food! One of my favorites, pambazos. It is a delicious blend of salsa, meat, cheese, and more. It is pretty messy, so I don't eat it too often. It's always a good day when I have a pambazo.
